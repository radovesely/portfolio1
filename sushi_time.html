TASK

Create interactive animation based on choose style.

PROCESS

My chosen style was based on Japanese characteriser designs, typical signs are for example big eyes, rounded head and small body. Usually these characters are little bit gothic and very cartoonish. My concept of this project was in Japanese style so I named my project Sushi time. The animation consist of different characters in love story when the main characters is taking the date into his favourite restaurant trying to guess her favourite food what is surprisingly sushi.
