TASK

Create responsive website with use of CSS grids about given style

PROCESS

My given style was the Optical art. The mix of patterns, lines and colors creating different optical illusions. My first question was how am I going to achieve to connected my type of style with given style. I started to do a research and collect pictures. I made mood board as a first visual idea what I kind of liked. The bigger issue was to create a logo, because for me was really difficult to sketch a logo with a touch of optical illusion. I came up with idea of the logo when I had to put my glasses on because my headstarted hurting after all these optical illusion. So I made a sketch of glasses with the well known illusionistic pattern. Next step was to vectorise it and make different versions.
